Immigration Programs - UK

Types of Visas

  • Skilled Worker Visa: To be eligible, applicants must have a job offer from a UK employer who is a licensed sponsor. They also need to meet specific skill and salary requirements.
  •  
  • Tier 2 Intra-Company Transfer Visa: This visa is for employees of multinational companies who are being transferred to a UK branch. There are different categories within this visa, such as long-term staff, graduate trainee, and short-term staff.
  •  
  • Tier 5 Temporary Worker Visa: This visa is for individuals coming to the UK for a short-term period to work or participate in cultural exchange programs.
  •  
  • Student Visa: International students can apply for this visa to study at a recognized educational institution in the UK. They need to have an offer of a place from a licensed sponsor and meet certain requirements.
  •  
  • Innovator Visa: This visa is for experienced businesspeople who want to establish an innovative business in the UK. Applicants need to have an innovative, viable, and scalable business idea endorsed by an approved endorsing body.
  •  
  • Start-up Visa: This visa is for individuals starting a new business in the UK for the first time. Applicants need to have an innovative, viable, and scalable business idea endorsed by an approved endorsing body.

Requirements

  • Skilled Worker Visa: Job offer, educational and professional qualifications, health and police clearance.
  •  
  • Tier 2 Intra-Company Transfer Visa: Dependent on the specific category within this visa.
  •  
  • Tier 5 Temporary Worker Visa: Dependent on the specific category within this visa.
  •  
  • Student Visa: Dependent on the specific requirements set by the educational institution and the visa category.
  •  
  • Innovator Visa: Innovative, viable, and scalable business idea endorsed by an approved endorsing body.
  •  
  • Start-up Visa: Innovative, viable, and scalable business idea endorsed by an approved endorsing body.

Application Process

  • Skilled Worker Visa: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy. Follow the specific guidance provided by the UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI).
  •  
  • Tier 2 Intra-Company Transfer Visa: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy. Follow the specific guidance provided by the UKVI.
  •  
  • Tier 5 Temporary Worker Visa: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy. Follow the specific guidance provided by the UKVI.
  •  
  • Student Visa: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy. Follow the specific guidance provided by the UKVI.
  •  
  • Innovator Visa: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy. Follow the specific guidance provided by the UKVI.
  •  
  • Start-up Visa: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy. Follow the specific guidance provided by the UKVI.

Common Steps Across All Visa Categories

  • Secure a Job/Admission: Ensure you have a job offer, business investment, or educational admission.
  •  
  • Prepare Documents: Gather necessary documents such as passports, photographs, qualifications, financial proof, and health reports.
  •  
  • Submit Application: Apply online or through the respective consulate/embassy.
  •  
  • Wait for Approval: Processing times vary; patience is crucial.
  •  
  • Travel and Registration: Once approved, travel to the UK and complete any in-country registration requirements.
